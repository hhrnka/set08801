Professional Tips

When baking a cheesecake, it is important not to over bake it. You only want to bake it unitl the middle has a slight wiggle, not until it is rock solid.

It is important that yo use a water bath, discussed at the right, to ensureeven baking of your cheesecake.

To create a water bath, use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfay up the sprng form pan in which the cake is placed.
